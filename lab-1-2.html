Edgar Allan Poe

The following is a quote from www.poets.org:
Edgar Allan Poe was born in Boston, Massachusetts, on January 19, 1809. Poe’s father and mother, both professional actors, died before the poet was three and John and Frances Allan raised him as a foster child in Richmond, Virginia.
John Allan, a prosperous tobacco exporter, sent Poe to the best boarding schools and later to the University of Virginia, where Poe excelled academically. After less than one year of school, however, he was forced to leave the University when Allan refused to pay his gambling debts.
